The Importance of Pigment Dispersion in Plastic Masterbatch

Why Dispersion Matters

Color Consistency: Poor dispersion leads to blotchy or streaky finishes, hurting product appeal.
Mechanical Integrity: Agglomerates (clumped pigments) weaken plastic structures, causing cracks or breaks.
Process Efficiency: Well-dispersed pigments flow smoothly during extrusion or molding, reducing downtime and waste.

GPA’s Edge in Dispersion Technology
